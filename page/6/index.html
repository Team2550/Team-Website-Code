About Skynet Robotics

At Skynet, we believe in nurturing future-ready innovators (engineers, programmers, builders, communicators) through hands-on robotics and teamwork. Whether you’re coding in C++, designing in CAD, strategizing a scouting campaign, or crafting a business plan, you’ll find your place on our team. No prior experience? No problem—our diverse roles mean there’s room for everyone.
